Gator Golfer

Welcome to the most anticipated game of all time!

Gator Golfer is a stimulating adventure in the wonderful world of mini-golf.

With state-of-the-art graphics and groundbreaking gameplay, you'll never stop putting!

Currently, there are 3 exciting levels to explore. Challenge yourself to see if you can get a hole in one!

COMING AT SOME POINT

Interactive Level Builder with drag-and-drop interface!

 

 

Controls

← Left Arrow
Aim the ball left
→ Right Arrow
Aim the ball right
↑ Up Arrow
Aim the ball up
_ Spacebar
Putt the ball
ESC Escape Key
Pause the game

In the top left of each level will be the title of the level.

In the top right of each level will be how many bounces you have left to get in the hole.
